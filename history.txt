early_life: Shivaji Bhosale was born on 19 February 1630 at Shivneri Fort. Father: Shahaji Bhosale, Mother: Jijabai. Family background: Bhosale Maratha clan.
childhood: Trained in martial arts, swordsmanship, archery, and horse riding. Inspired by Ramayana and Mahabharata.
swarajya: Lifelong mission was self-rule. Captured Torana Fort at age 16. Consolidated territories to form Maratha state.
military: Guerrilla warfare expert. Captured Raigad, Pratapgad, Sindhudurg, Rajgad, Torna. Built navy. Key battles: Pratapgad, against Mughals and Bijapur.
administration: Declared Chhatrapati in 1674. Ashta Pradhan Mandal (8 ministers). Revenue system: Chauth, Sardeshmukhi. Law, justice, religious tolerance.
personal_life: Married Saibai Nimbalkar, Sakvarbai, Putalabai. Children: Sambhaji Maharaj. Known for courage, strategy, justice, frugality, and devotion to Bhavani Mata.
